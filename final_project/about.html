About Liam Ryan

Education:

Growing up, my parents believed that receiving a strong education was the best thing they could give me, therefore after pre-school I went to a christian private school called Bear Creek from K-4 and then moved to complete my middle and high school education at Eastside Preparatory School. I am currently in my senior year, and am set to graduate and attend Carnegie Mellon University for all four years of college in Fall 2022.

Web Design:

I have been doing Web Design since it became a passion of mine the summer before 8th grade. I took an online course throughout that summer and the concept of creating my own websites grasped my interest quickly. From there, I signed up for a required WEB class during school, then took a Web Design 1 and Web Design 2 class as electives throughout high school. I thoroughly enjoy it and hope to continue into college and perhaps after.

Hobbies:

Some of my hobbies include Soccer, which I play at a club, hs, and college level. I invest a lot of my time into athletics such as this and ultimate frisbee. Additionally, I play piano (used to be competitively), clarinet, and saxophone. I also enjoy programming and playing videogames such as Call of Duty and FIFA.

Future Interests:

In the future, I'd like to pursue a career in the tech industry. Although I would like to go to a professional level in soccer before this, within 4 years of leaving college I'd like to begin working at a major tech company. I am going to Carnegie Mellon enrolled in the Information Systems department. This will allow me to pursue a more detailed subject once I have enough information to make a full choice on my career.
